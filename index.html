popular destinations in EGYPT

Giza City
Giza is a city in Egypt, located on the west bank of the Nile River, just south of Cairo. It is famous for the Giza Plateau, which is home to the Great Sphinx and the Pyramids of Giza, three of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.
Luxor City
The city of Luxor is a popular tourist destination, and is known as the "world's greatest open-air museum." Visitors can explore the ancient temples and tombs, or take a felucca ride down the Nile River. Luxor is also a major cultural center, and is home to a number of museums, art galleries, and theaters.
Sinai peninsulla
The Sinai Peninsula has a long and rich history, dating back to the Paleolithic era. It was an important crossroads for ancient civilizations, including the Egyptians, the Israelites, and the Romans
